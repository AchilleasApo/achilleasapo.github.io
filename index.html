About

Hi, I'm Achilleas Apostolou and I'm a 3D Technical Designer.

I was born and raised in Athens, Greece in 1997 and have since then lived in Heraklion, Geneva and Copenhagen. My passion for cinema, digital arts, and innovative technologies led me to earn a BSc in Computer Science from the University of Crete, specializing in Computer Graphics, and an MSc in Medialogy at Aalborg University Copenhagen, focusing on real-time graphics and virtual production. In my free time I enjoy sailing, bouldering, traveling and meeting new people.
