Overview

Overview

Blurspot Films was created by a bunch of creative techies in order to bring their dreams to life.

Blurspot Films was created by Nick Pattas and Achilleas Apostolou in 2021 in Heraklion Crete as a continuation of their first filmmaking projects: Déjà Rêvé (2019) and Pythmenas (2020). Blurspot is now based in Athens after the addition of Vasilis Tsimitras to the team.
